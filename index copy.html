ShriTeq 2018

The Shri Ram School, Moulsari Campus is proud to present ShriTeq, the only technology symposium of its kind. Built on the success of three previous iterations, ShriTeq 2017 is on a larger and grander stage than ever before. ShriTeq consists of four events: the Technology Quiz, the Hackathon, Scratch Animation, and our moviemaking event, Lights, Camera, Action! Our flagship event, the Technology Quiz hosted by tech guru Rajiv Makhni, is North Indiaâ€™s largest tech quiz at the school level. Participants in the Hackathon will be challenged to innovate and create high-quality products by harnessing the power of technology, while the creative events will challenge participants to combine creativity and technical skill to intertwine art and technology. Featuring over 25 prestigious schools in the Delhi-NCR, ShriTeq promises to be the experience of a lifetime.

Dates:

Preliminary Round(s) Monday, 1st October
Semi Finals & Final Round Tusday, 9th October


Procedures:

The teams will go through a preliminary round(s) on 1st October 2018. This year, there will be two rounds. The first round will start at 9:45 am sharp.
The top-ten teams in the first preliminary round would directly qualify for the semifinals.
A second round would be conducted with the schools who do not qualify. The top two teams from this round will join the other teams making it a twelve-team semifinal roster. Two teams will be wait-listed and contacted in the case of a dropout.
The shortlisted teams will proceed to the semi-finals to be held on 9th October 2017.
Six teams emerging out of the Semi Final rounds would battle it out for the winning trophy in the Finals to be held on the same day.


Participation Details

Number of teams per school: 1
Number of participants per team: 3
Eligibility: Students of class 5
Students must carry their school Identity cards.


Quiz Topics:

IT companies: their innovative ideas, logos, tag lines, software products, people, new trends, legends etc.
World of internet: unique websites, IT buzzwords and acronyms / abbreviations etc.
Games and Gadgets
History of IT, software versions, hardware evolution
Emerging terms and trends e.g. Cloud computing, net neutrality, telecom, biometrics, robotics, artificial intelligence, virtual reality, gaming etc.
